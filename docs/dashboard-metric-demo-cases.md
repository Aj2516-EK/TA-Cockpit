# Dashboard Metric Demo Cases

## Readiness

### Qualified Candidates Availability
- Compare two business units on critical-skill roles to show talent depth risk.
- Compare two locations for qualified-candidate supply.
- Compare source mixes (e.g., LinkedIn vs Referral-heavy slice) for qualified-per-req impact.

### Talent Pool Skill Readiness
- Internal vs External readiness comparison.
- Role family comparison to identify low-readiness roles.
- Department comparison to prioritize upskilling or better screening.

### Active External Connections
- Recruiter comparison for external network strength.
- Source comparison to identify strongest external channels.
- Department-level external coverage check for upcoming hiring demand.

### Time to Present Critical Skills
- Department comparison for critical-skill shortlist speed.
- Location comparison for market availability constraints.
- Critical-skill-only slice to show SLA breach risk.

### Critical Skill Hiring Capability
- Compare departments on percent of critical requisitions with hires.
- Compare this quarter vs previous quarter with same filters.
- Compare high-volume vs low-volume critical roles for conversion reliability.

### Talent Pool Size & Variety
- Department comparison for pool breadth.
- Role comparison to find narrow talent pools.
- Source diversity check to detect channel over-reliance.
